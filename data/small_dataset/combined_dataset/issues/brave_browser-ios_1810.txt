'Look Up' Causes a Page Refresh


Description:
Looking up a text selection causes the page to refresh (clearing populated form fields, etc).
Steps to Reproduce
Go to any page and make a selection.
From the selection context menu, select Look Up.
Dismiss the look-up window.
Actual result:
Page is refreshed.
Expected result:
Page should not refresh.
Reproduces how often:
Easily reproduced.
Brave Version:
1.6.6 (18.11.15.17)
Device details:
iPhone 6s+
Additional Information
Reported on Twitter.
