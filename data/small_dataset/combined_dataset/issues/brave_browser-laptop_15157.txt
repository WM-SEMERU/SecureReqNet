Autotype Username writing in Password field


Description
When you have a remembered Username and click tab it types it in the Password field instead of the Username field
Steps to Reproduce

Go to https://pastebin.com/login or any other place to login
Start typing your Username (login and out if none)
Press Tab when it shows your username as remembered

Actual result:
Username gets entered in the Password field
Expected result:
Username in the Username field
Reproduces how often:
100%
