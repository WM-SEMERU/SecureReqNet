PDFs are still opened in-browser even though "Download PDF files instead of automatically opening them in Brave" is on


Description
When I attempt to download any .pdf file, the file is opened by the built in brave PDF viewer even though "Download PDF files instead of automatically opening them in Brave" is turned on (Settings > Advanced Settings > Content Settings > PDF documents).
Steps to Reproduce

Turn "Download PDF files instead of automatically opening them in Brave" on;
Close Browser
Reopen Browser
Open any link to a pdf
ex: http://www.africau.edu/images/default/sample.pdf

What version of Brave are you using?
Version 0.56.15 Chromium: 70.0.3538.110 (Official Build) (64-bit)
