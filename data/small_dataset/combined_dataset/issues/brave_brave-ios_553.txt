Do not hide address bar on navigation


Description:
Hiding URL bar on navigation can be used by phishing actors.
See here: https://www.securitee.org/files/hindsight_ccs2017.pdf (ABB 15-16)
Steps to Reproduce

Navigate to reddit.com
Scroll

Actual result: 
Scrolling hides the address bar
Expected result:
URL bar should be visible at all times
Reproduces how often: [Easily reproduced, Intermittent Issue]
Everytime
Brave Version: 
1.7 (18.11.30.03)
Device details: 
iPhone SE | iOS 12.1
