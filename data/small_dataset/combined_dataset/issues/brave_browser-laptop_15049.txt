Bitwarden extension icon does not move focus to 'Search vault' field when pressed.


Description
Bitwarden extension icon does not move focus to 'Search vault' field when pressed.
Steps to Reproduce

Be logged in to Bitwarden.
Activate Bitwarden by pressing the extension icon top right.

Actual result:
'Search vault' field is not in focus, as it should be.
Expected result:
'Search vault' field is in focus and searching for passwords can immediately begin. This behavior is seen when the same actions are taken in other browsers' Bitwarden extensions.
Reproduces how often:
Always.
