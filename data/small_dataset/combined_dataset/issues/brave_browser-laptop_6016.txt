https://apple.com shows cert error for some users in Brave


Navigating to apple.com in the navigation bar expands the address to https://apple.com, which fails certificate validation:

Navigating to www.apple.com expands the address to https://www.apple.com, which passes certificate validation.
Navigating to https://apple.com in Chrome works, so it appears that Crome follows the redirect before validating the cert.
